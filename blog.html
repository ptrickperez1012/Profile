Prima Pasta Badminton
In partnership with the Philippine Badminton Association , the annual event is part of the Philippine National Ranking System (PNRS), wherein aspirants in the Open Division will be able to earn points that will be the basis for their position in the national ranking. The tournament, sponsored by Babolat and Smart Communications through MVP Sports Foundation, will include these events: men’s doubles and mixed doubles from open class, to levels A to G, while open class and levels B to F will be available in the women’s doubles. The event is also supported by Boysen Paints, Morning Star Milling Corporation., Mabz Builders, ILO Construction, Monolith Construction, Monocrete Construction, Pioneer Insurance, Promax International, Regent Foods Corp., and RFM Corp. and organized by Forthright Events.
